Labour party

Labour party is a social democratic party built on the values of freedom, equality and solidarity. our goal is the greatest possible freedom for every individual everyone should have equal opportunities regardless of where you live and how much money you have. Everyone must have a job to go to and a wage to live on. We must cut climate emissions and create new jobs. They will ensure security for people, create opportunities and solve tasks together. The Labor Party believes that they do more together than individually. Work for everyone Everyone must have a job that provides enough money to live a good life. They want to create new future- oriented jobs in green industries, get more young people into work and ensure that there are more full, permanent positions. Norwegian climate goals must be reached! They need a fair climate policy which cuts emissions and takes care of nature.They want to do the society more green and environmentally friendly and develop the new Labour Party for the future.
