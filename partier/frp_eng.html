Progress Party

You and your family must have security and freedom of choice in everyday life, and it is you and not politicians who must decide which school you will attend. The progress party will build more pedestrian and cycle paths, especially along school roads, and the progress part will reduce the age limit for mopeds to 15 years, and for cars to 17 years.
