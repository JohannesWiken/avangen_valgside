Christian Democratic Party

The most important thing for Cpp is that you as a parent should have time and space to be with your children. We must have a healthcare system with time to provide safe care and competent help when you need it. Human dignity, which means that all people are equally valuable, regardless of who you are and what abilities you have. Stewardship responsibility, which means that we must take care of our planet and the people who live there. We must reduce greenhouse gas emissions and take care of our earth.

School

More about CDP

Kristelig Folkeparti is a Christian democratic political party in Norway that was created in 1933. Olaug Bollestad has been party leader since 2021 and after that it was Kjell Inge Robstad. Dag kjel Inge Ulstein is another deputy leader at the national meeting in 2023, Ingunn Ulf was appointed new general secretary in February 2023. That profile was strengthened in the 1980s and 1990s, especially ready for it is Kåre Kristiansen, who was party leader in some of the 1970s and 1980s.
